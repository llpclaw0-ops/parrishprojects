Privacy Policy

This policy explains, in plain English, what information Parrish Projects collects when you use this website, why, and what your rights are. We keep it simple because we collect very little: essentially, only what you choose to send us through the contact form.

Last updated: 24 June 2026

Who we are

Parrish Projects ("we", "us", "our") is a systems studio based in Guernsey, Channel Islands. For any question about this policy or your information, contact us at lewis@parrishprojects.com.

For the purposes of data protection law, Parrish Projects is the data controller for the information you submit through this website.

What information we collect

We only collect the information you actively give us. When you fill in the contact form, that includes your name, your email address, and any details you choose to write in your message, such as your budget range, timeline, what you need, and how you prefer to be contacted.

This is a static website. We do not set first-party advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not run analytics or build a behavioural profile of you. We do not knowingly collect any special category data, and you should not send us sensitive personal information through the form.

How we use it

  • To read and respond to your enquiry.
  • To discuss whether and how we can help with your project.
  • To keep a record of our correspondence so we can follow up properly.

We use your information only for these purposes. We will not use it for unrelated marketing, and we will not sell it to anyone, ever.

Our lawful basis

We process your contact-form information on the basis of our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries about our services, and to take steps at your request before potentially entering into a contract. Where the law requires consent, the act of submitting the form is your request for us to get in touch.

Who handles it (our processors)

The contact form is delivered to us by a third-party form provider, FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), which passes your message to our email inbox. Your message is also stored in our email provider so we can read and reply to it. These providers process the data on our behalf, only to deliver and store your message.

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. As with any website, the host may process limited technical request data (such as IP address) in server logs for security and delivery, which is standard for serving any web page.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry correspondence only for as long as it is useful: while we are in contact, and for a reasonable period afterwards in case you come back to us. If you would like us to delete your enquiry and our correspondence, just ask and we will.

International transfers

Some of our providers (for example our form and email providers) may process data on servers outside the Bailiwick of Guernsey, including in the UK, EU or US. Where that happens, we rely on those providers offering appropriate, recognised safeguards for the data.

Your rights

You have rights over your personal information, including the right to ask for a copy of what we hold, to have it corrected, to have it deleted, and to object to or restrict how we use it. To exercise any of these, email us at lewis@parrishprojects.com and we will respond.

Parrish Projects operates under The Data Protection (Bailiwick of Guernsey) Law, 2017. If you are in the UK or EU, equivalent rights under UK GDPR and EU GDPR are respected. If you are unhappy with how we have handled your information, you can complain to the Office of the Data Protection Authority (ODPA) in Guernsey at odpa.gg, or to your local supervisory authority.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it was last changed. If we make a significant change, the updated version will always be the one published here.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear, just ask. We'd rather you understood it.

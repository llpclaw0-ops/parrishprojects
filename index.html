How we work

  1. Discover: We map how your business actually works today: the manual steps, the leaks, the bottlenecks, then agree what "good" looks like.
  2. Design: We architect the system: the flows, the data model, the interfaces. You see exactly what we will build before a line of code.
  3. Build: Senior engineering, shipped in tight increments. You watch it come together, with no black boxes and no six-month silence.
  4. Test: We pressure-test against real data and real edge cases. If it can break, we break it here, not in front of your customers.
  5. Launch: We roll it out carefully, train your team, and stand by while the first real traffic flows through the system.
  6. Improve: Systems are never "done". We watch the numbers, find the next bottleneck, and keep compounding the gains.

Frequently asked questions

What does Parrish Projects do?

We are a systems studio. We design and build smart automation, web and app products, integrations, lead capture and recovery systems, and operational dashboards, then connect them into one clean system your business runs on.

How much does a project cost?

Most projects fall between £1k and £40k or more, depending on scope. Smaller automations and tools sit at the lower end; full systems with integrations, dashboards and ongoing care sit higher. We give a clear estimate before any work starts.

How quickly can you get something live?

We aim to have your first system live within about 30 days of kickoff. We ship in tight increments rather than disappearing for months, so you see it come together and can use it sooner.

Do I own the code and the system?

Yes. Everything we build is clean, documented and handover-ready. The system is yours to own, code included. You are never locked behind us, and you can take it elsewhere if you ever need to.

What tools and technologies do you build with?

We work across React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node, Python and Postgres, with OpenAI and Anthropic for AI, Stripe and Twilio for payments and messaging, and automation platforms like n8n, Zapier and Airtable. We pick what fits your operation.

Do you work with businesses outside the UK?

Yes. We work remotely on UK and EU hours and build systems for businesses across the United Kingdom and Europe. The whole process, from discovery to launch and ongoing support, runs smoothly without needing to be in the same room.

What is your process for a new project?

Six clear stages: Discover how your business works today, Design the system, Build it in tight increments, Test against real data, Launch carefully with your team, then Improve continuously. You see exactly what we will build before any code is written.

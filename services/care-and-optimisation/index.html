Care & Support

Ongoing care that keeps your systems healthy and improving

Software is never truly finished. Things change, businesses grow, and systems need to keep up. We stay on after launch to monitor, maintain and improve what we have built, so it keeps getting better instead of quietly decaying.

The problem we fix

Most builds are abandoned the moment they launch, then slowly rot: small bugs pile up, performance slips, and the next bottleneck never gets fixed. Ongoing care keeps the system healthy, catches problems early, and keeps finding the next improvement, so your software compounds in value rather than degrading.

What you get

The result is software you do not have to worry about: monitored, maintained, and steadily improving. We are honest about scope and progress, with no vague retainers, so you always know what you are paying for and what it is doing.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to take ongoing support?

No. Your system is documented, portable and supportable. Ongoing care is there if you want a reliable partner watching and improving the system, not a lock-in.

What does ongoing support actually include?

Monitoring so issues are caught early, maintenance to keep things secure and current, and continuous optimisation to keep improving speed, reliability and results. We are clear about scope so there are no vague retainers.

Can you support a system you did not build?

Often yes. We would start by reviewing what you have, telling you honestly what shape it is in, and then agreeing what is worth maintaining and improving.
